# Privacy Policy for BREAK!!-Online

**Last Updated:** February 24, 2026

YortorGames ("we," "us," or "our") operates the BREAK!!-Online mobile game (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Game.

By using BREAK!!-Online, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you sign in to BREAK!!-Online using Google Sign-In, we collect:
- **Name** - Your Google display name
- **Email Address** - Your Google email
- **Profile Photo** - Your Google profile picture
- **User ID** - A unique identifier from Google

### 1.2 Game Data
We automatically collect:
- **Game Scores** - Your gameplay scores and rankings
- **Gameplay Statistics** - Number of games played, high scores, achievements
- **Custom Username** - If you set a custom display name in the game
- **Game Mode Preferences** - Whether you play Classic or Ranked mode

### 1.3 Device Information
We may collect:
- **Device Type** - Model and manufacturer
- **Operating System** - Android version
- **App Version** - Which version of BREAK!!-Online you're using
- **Crash Reports** - Technical data if the game crashes

### 1.4 Information We DO NOT Collect
- **Location data** - We do not track your location
- **Contacts** - We do not access your contacts
- **Photos/Media** - We do not access your device storage
- **Payment information** - The game is free with no in-app purchases
- **Browsing history** - We do not track your activity outside the game

- Authenticate your account via Google Sign-In

- Analyze gameplay patterns to improve game balance

- Develop new features based on player feedback
- Optimize game performance

### 2.3 To Ensure Fair Play
- Detect and prevent cheating

### 2.4 To Communicate With You
- Send important game updates and announcements
- Respond to support requests
- Notify you of new features or events

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 Public Information
**When you play Ranked Mode:**
- Your **custom username** (or Google display name)
- Your **profile photo**
- Your **game scores**
- Your **rank**

These are visible to ALL players on the global leaderboard.

**When you play Classic Mode:**
- Your scores are stored locally on your device
- No information is shared publicly

### 3.2 Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:

**Google Sign-In (Google LLC)**
- Purpose: Account authentication
- Data Shared: Name, email, profile photo, user ID
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Firebase (Google LLC)**
- Purpose: Data storage, authentication, crash reporting
- Data Shared: User data, game scores, device information
- Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### 3.3 What We DO NOT Share
- We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties
- We do NOT share your email address with other players
- We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes
- We do NOT share your information for marketing purposes

### 3.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations or court orders
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud or illegal activities
- Protect the safety of users

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored
Your data is stored securely using Firebase services provided by Google Cloud Platform. Servers may be located in various countries, but all comply with applicable data protection laws.

### 4.2 How We Protect Your Data
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption** - Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- **Secure Authentication** - Google Sign-In uses OAuth 2.0
- **Access Controls** - Limited employee access to user data
- **Regular Security Audits** - Ongoing monitoring for vulnerabilities
- **Firebase Security Rules** - Database access is restricted

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts:** Data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Data may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access Your Data
You can view your information in the game:
- **Settings** → View your email and username
- **Leaderboard** → See your public profile and scores

### 5.2 Update Your Data
- **Username:** Change your display name in Settings
- **Profile Photo:** Update via your Google account settings
- **Email:** Update via your Google account settings

### 5.3 Delete Your Data
To delete your data:
1. Sign in to BREAK!!-Online
2. Go to **Settings** → **Sign Out**
3. Uninstall the game
4. Email us at privacy@yortorgames.com with your request

We will delete your data within **30 days**. Note that some information may be retained in backups for a limited period.

### 5.4 Opt-Out of Leaderboards
You can opt-out of public leaderboards by:
- Only playing **Classic Mode** (not Ranked Mode)
- Classic Mode scores are stored locally and not shared

### 5.5 Data Portability
You may request a copy of your data by emailing privacy@yortorgames.com. We will provide your data in a machine-readable format within 30 days.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectify inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@yortorgames.com.

### 7.2 California Users (CCPA)
If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@yortorgames.com.
