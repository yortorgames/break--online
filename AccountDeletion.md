# Account & Data Deletion Request

## BREAK!!-Online by YortorGames

### How to Request Account Deletion

1. **Email us at:** yortorgames@gmail.com
2. **Subject line:** "Account Deletion Request - BREAK!!-Online"
3. **Include in your email:**
   - Your Google account email address
   - Your in-game username (if known)
   - Request for complete account and data deletion

### What Data Will Be Deleted

- ✅ Firebase Firestore leaderboard data (scores, rank)
- ✅ Profile information (display name, photo URL)
- ✅ All game statistics and history

### Data Retention Period

- Deletion requests are processed within **30 days**
- Some data may be retained for up to **90 days** for backup purposes
- AdMob/analytics data is managed by Google and subject to their policies

### Contact

For questions about data deletion, contact: yortorgames@gmail.com
